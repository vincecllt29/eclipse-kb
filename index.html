Returns & Exchanges
What is your return policy?
All sales are final. However, if you shop online, you can purchase Final Sale Return Coverage at checkout, which gives you the option to return items for a refund or store credit. Return requests must be filed within 7 days of receiving the product.
Can I return my item?
Only if you shopped online and purchased Final Sale Return Coverage at checkout. If you didn't, your order is final sale — no exceptions. Return requests must be made within 7 days of delivery via our customer portal.
Can I cancel my order?
No — all sales are final and orders cannot be cancelled once placed.
Can I exchange an item for a different size or color?
No — all items are final sale. Exchanges are not offered unless Final Sale Return Coverage was purchased at checkout.
Can I return something I bought in-store?
No — all in-store purchases are final sale with no returns or exchanges.
I received the wrong item — what do I do?
We're sorry about that! Email contact@eclipse-official.com with a photo of the item you received and the name of the item that was supposed to be included. We'll arrange a replacement if available, or issue a full refund with a prepaid return label. You have a 7-day window from delivery to report this.
My order was incomplete / an item is missing — what do I do?
First check whether a partial shipment was sent — some orders ship in multiple parts. If an item is truly missing, email contact@eclipse-official.com with your order number and the missing item name within 7 days of delivery.
I received a damaged item — what do I do?
We apologize! Email contact@eclipse-official.com with a photo of the damaged item within 7 days of delivery. We'll replace it if available, or issue a full refund with a prepaid return label.
